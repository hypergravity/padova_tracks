Track Interpolations

PARSEC tracks are interpolated such that each track has the same number of points between each equivallent evolutionary point (EEP).

Line number* EEP Notes
0 Beginning of Pre Main Sequence
80 Minimum of Pre Main Sequence
160 End of Pre-Main Sequence
240 Beginning of Main Sequence (MS): Starting of the central H-burning. For M > 20 Msun: Log L minimum after end of PMS.
439 Log Teff minimum on the Main Sequence. Calculated one of four possible ways:
  1. First Minimum in Teff
  2. For no minimum Log Teff (M>50, usually >100), halfway in age between MS beginning and MS Turn off
  3. Xc=0.30 (Central H mass fraction) for low-mass stars (via BaSTi)
  4. For very low mass stars that never reach Xc=0.30 half the age of the universe ~= 13.7/2 Gyr
539 Main Sequence turn off: log Teff maximum along the Main Sequence For very low mass stars that never reach the MSTO: the age of the universe ~= 13.7 Gyr
639 Max luminosity of the Sub-giant phase: Maximum in log L for high-mass or Xc=0.0 for low-mass stars (via BaSTi)
709 Base of the Red Giant (RG) Branch: Minimum in log L for high-mass or base of the RGB for low-mass stars (via BaSTi)
1079 RG bump 1: The maximum luminosity during the RGB Bump
1109 RG bump 2: The minimum luminosity during the RGB Bump
1509 RG tip: Tip of the RGB (TRGB) defined in 3 ways:
  1. If the last track model still has a He center abundance (YCEN) > 0.1 the TRGB is either the minimum log Teff or the last model, which ever comes first. (low masses)
  2. If YCEN==0 at the last track model, TRGB is the minimum log Teff where YCEN > 1-Z-0.1.
  3. If there is still H in the center (very low mass), TRGB is the final track model point.
1549 Beginning He Fusion: Start quiescent central He-burning phase Minimum of the luminosity due to He fusion after the TRGB, which dips as the star contracts, and then ramps up.
1699 YCEN=0.550 Central abundance of He equal to 0.550
1799 YCEN=0.500 Central abundance of He equal to 0.500
1859 YCEN=0.400 Central abundance of He equal to 0.400
1959 YCEN=0.200 Central abundance of He equal to 0.200
2039 YCEN=0.100 Central abundance of He equal to 0.100
2119 YCEN=0.005 Central abundance of He equal to 0.005
2199 YCEN=0.000 Central abundance of He equal to 0.000
2279 AGB EEP 1: Helium (shell) fusion first overpowers hydrogen (shell) fusion
2359 AGB EEP 2: Hydrogen wins again (before TPAGB).
2439 TPAGB Starting of the central C-burning phase or beginning of TPAGB.

Helium burning phases take place from 1549 on. There are four different track lengths:

  1. 539: Up to the MS turn off (low mass)
  2. 1509: Up to the TRGB (He-flash stars that will continue onto the horizontal branch)
  3. 2439: All phases (intermediate high mass)
  4. 890: Only Helium burning phases (Horizontal Branch tracks)

* numbers quoted do not include header line of the file so they should be 1 off from the line number or e.g., wc *.dat

File Format

Within each metallicity set (directory) there are a number of tracks (files). The file names are formated by initial metallicity, helium, and mass (Z{}_Y{}_M{}.extension). Horizontal branch tracks are calculated seperately and have extenstion .HB.dat all other tracks have only the .dat extention

Each file has six columns: log Age, Mass, log Te, Mbol, log g, C/O in units yrs, Msun, K, bolometric mag, solar surface gravity, and unitless.

Notes on column formats:

  • Horizontal branch ages start at the zero age horizontal branch.
  • C/O is currently a place holder until I add the TP-AGB phases.
  • PARSEC does not calculate log g or Mbol they are calculated here from Log Teff, Log L and Mass:
    Mbol = 4.77 - 2.5 * log L
    log g = -10.616 + log mass + 4.0 * log Teff - log L
(all logs are base 10)
